Scoring formula (Manual section 3.6)
Climb multiplier = 1 + Σ(increment of every team on the alliance) None = 0 · Contact = +0.05 · Zone 1 = +0.10 · Zone 2 = +0.20 · Zone 3 = +0.30 Alliance score = round(Contained Wildfire × Climb multiplier) + Partner Climb (25 per team supported by a partner) + Extinguisher (shared, 1 point per WILDFIRE) + Coopertition Bonus (shared: 4→10 / 5→25 / 6→40) + penalties awarded from the opponent (0 points for the match with a white/red card)

Extinguisher and Coopertition Bonus are earned by the single 6-team Global Alliance and are added equally to both regional alliances. A minor foul transfers 5% of the offending alliance's score to its opponent, a major foul transfers 10%. All fractional scores are rounded to the nearest whole number.
